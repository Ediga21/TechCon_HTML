Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meet-up for software developers and quickly evolved into one of the most anticipated global tech summits. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees, introduced cutting-edge technologies, and launched the careers of several renowned tech leaders.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we believe technology can empower humanity. Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and inspiration among developers, entrepreneurs, and leaders who are shaping the digital future.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lin – AI Researcher

Ada has been at the forefront of ethical AI and has developed frameworks adopted by major global firms. Her keynote at TechCon 2022 inspired a shift toward more responsible machine learning systems.

Jamal Roberts – Cloud Computing Pioneer

Jamal’s work in serverless architecture revolutionized how we scale applications. He’s a two-time TechCon speaker and continues to mentor emerging tech startups worldwide.
